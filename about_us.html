ABOUT US

The Team Behind the AI Dance Project

Hello! Our names are Ava and Jinn. We are students at St. Olaf College, which is located in South-Central Minnesota. We are both Computer Science students who developed this project for our Ethics of Software Design class. Generative AI is being increasingly used to create works, and this increasing prevalence has led to our interest in exploring the ethical concerns that arise when creating something with this software.
