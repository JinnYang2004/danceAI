CAN CHATGPT CREATE A DANCE?

ABSTRACT OF OUR WORK

With the growing popularity of generative AI, this software has begun to work its way into the dance community. Gen AI software such as Chat GPT has become increasingly used to generate ideas for choreography. Our team explored this question by creating a dance with ChatGPT. Experimenting directly with the source provided our team with clear insight into the cons as well as the pros of creating a dance with AI.
